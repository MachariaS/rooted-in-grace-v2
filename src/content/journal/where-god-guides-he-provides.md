There is a quiet comfort in knowing that God never calls us into purpose and then abandons us along the way.

Sometimes we worry about what we don't have the resources, the connections, the finances, or the experience. We convince ourselves that we must first gather everything before we can begin.

But God often works the other way around.

He asks us to take a step of faith, and then, one by one, He places the right people, the right opportunities, and the right provisions in our path. Not always when we expect them, but always when we need them.

Purpose has a way of attracting God's provision.

That doesn't mean the journey will be easy or free from challenges. It means that when your heart is surrendered to His will, you are never walking alone. Heaven has a way of making room for what God has ordained.

If God planted the vision in your heart, trust Him with the process. Keep showing up. Keep serving faithfully. Keep taking the next step.

The same God who gave you the assignment is more than able to provide what is needed to accomplish it.

Perhaps today's reminder is simply this:

**Don't let the absence of resources stop you from pursuing what God has called you to do. Often, provision is already on its way.**

*"And my God will meet all your needs according to the riches of His glory in Christ Jesus."* 

**Philippians 4:19**
